About The Law Office of Gary S. Austin

About Us

Gary S. Austin is a trusted trial attorney and the founder of the Law Office of Gary S. Austin, located in Riverside, CA. With more than 20 years of experience in litigation and negotiation, Mr. Austin brings a practical, solutions-oriented approach to legal challenges across family law, civil disputes, and employment law.

He is known for delivering strategic clarity to complex matters, whether representing clients in divorce, custody disputes, domestic violence restraining orders, or high-stakes business and contract cases. Mr. Austin has served as lead trial counsel in state and federal courts, earning a reputation for calm, persuasive courtroom advocacy and thorough case preparation.

Prior to founding his own practice in 2010, he spent a decade at the esteemed Law Office of Carl E. Douglas in Beverly Hills, where he litigated high-profile civil rights and wrongful death cases, including multimillion-dollar jury trials. He also served as a legal intern in the Los Angeles County District Attorney’s Office, gaining early trial experience that continues to inform his direct and effective litigation style.

Mission Statement:
